# Credit Card Customer Segmentation
This is an end-to-end customer clustering project that groups credit card customers into distinct segments based on their demographic and transactional behavior. The notebook explores the dataset, performs preprocessing and exploratory data analysis, evaluates clustering performance with KMeans, and uses PCA to reduce dimensionality and improve interpretability.

## What the notebook covers
- Loads and inspects the credit card customer dataset.
- Performs exploratory data analysis on numerical and categorical features.
- Checks for missing values and summarizes the dataset statistics.
- Visualizes feature distributions and relationships to understand customer behavior.
- Applies KMeans clustering to segment customers into groups.
- Uses the elbow method and silhouette scores to help choose the number of clusters.
- Applies PCA to reduce dimensionality and assess whether clustering can be improved.

## Outcome
The customers are divided into 6 groups, based on their history with the bank, with highlighted traits, and further suggestions to increase business and minimize risks.
